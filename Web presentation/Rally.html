Rally Car Selection

Welcome to the Rally Car Selection Challenge! Here, you can explore some of the most exciting rally cars and choose your favorite one from the list below.

Steps to Begin Your Rally

  1. Look at the rally cars.
  2. Read about their features below.
  3. Choose your favorite rally car from the dropdown menu.
  4. Press “Submit Choice” to see who drives it!
  5. Get ready to race!

Rally Car Gallery

Subaru WRX STI

  • Turbocharged 2.0L flat-four engine.
  • Legendary all-wheel-drive system.
  • Iconic blue and gold rally livery.
  • Strong handling and cornering grip.
  • Famous for its success in WRC history.

Ford Fiesta WRC

  • 1.6L EcoBoost turbocharged engine.
  • Developed by M-Sport in the UK.
  • Lightweight carbon-fiber body panels.
  • Known for quick acceleration and agility.
  • Frequently seen in WRC podium finishes.

Toyota Yaris GR

  • Based on Toyota’s WRC-winning design.
  • Powered by a 1.6L 3-cylinder turbo engine.
  • Uses Toyota’s GR-Four AWD system.
  • Compact yet extremely powerful for its size.
  • Praised for precise handling and reliability.

Hyundai i20 Coupe WRC

  • Turbocharged 1.6L inline-four engine.
  • Features advanced aerodynamics.
  • Developed by Hyundai Motorsport in Germany.
  • Highly competitive in modern WRC events.
  • Noted for stability at high speeds.

Citroën C3 WRC

  • Built for high-downforce performance.
  • Turbocharged 1.6L direct-injection engine.
  • Lightweight chassis and powerful braking system.
  • Efficient suspension for mixed terrain.
  • Strong legacy in World Rally Championship.
